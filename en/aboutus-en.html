What is IDs Club?

This is a collaborative community, not just a meet up group. Our club is largest Asian community of developers,creatives, designers, entrepreneurs, startups and others working together. Got an idea for the next great mobile app, web service, or product, If you are ambitious and hungry for success,this is the place for you, IDs.

Also, it is a non-profit and self-organised monthly event (Ideas Sunday) aimed at bringing together the creative Asian within Australia, discuss and learn about all things developer, UX design, startups related.

Are you a graphic/industrial designer, mobile or web developer? Are you an independent contractor, entrepreneur, or small business owner? If so, this is where you need to be. If you are an investor looking for opportunities, these are the people you may want to meet. Want to contribute or create a new start up venture, here is your opportunity.

IDs was established to fill a niche that Asian community in Australia. We are here to help members learn, identify resources and move forward with their projects. Again, we are building a community of member who want to learn, share, collaborate and grow. We are about quality not quantity in membership rolls. We want our members to succeed, and we will do everything possible to accomplish that goal.

Sure, we will share and discuss topics like any other meet up group at First Sunday of every month (Call it Ideas Sunday), but in most cases they will be relevant to projects that member are actually working on. There will be times to socialise too.

Developing a great solution requires hard work, dedication and help from many talented people who offer different pieces to the puzzle. Great solutions are comprised of many building blocks which require expertise and experience that some individuals alone do not possess.
